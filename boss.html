给决策层 · 两分钟For decision-makers · 2 min

新平台值不值得，差异化成不成立？Is the new platform worth it, and does its differentiation hold?

我们用一套公平的对照测试，把"感觉"变成"证据"——同样的输入、喂给两个平台、由多名测试员打分，最后给你一个有据可依的结论。A fair, controlled comparison turns "gut feel" into evidence — identical inputs fed to both platforms, scored by several testers, ending in a conclusion you can stand behind.

我们要回答的核心问题The core questions

开发者做新平台，最明确的一个目标是"给用户更多可编辑的自由"——剧本和图片都能更强地改。这是他们的赌注，也是这次测试的靶心。其余几条还没有定论，由数据来回答。The developer's clearest goal for the new platform is "more editing freedom for users" — stronger editing of both script and images. That's their bet and the bullseye of this test. The rest are still open, to be answered by data.

H1
Oppy 给用户更多可编辑自由Oppy gives users more editing freedom
到底成不成立？自由体现在哪？代价是什么？——重点验证。Does it hold? Where does the freedom show? At what cost? — the primary focus.

两个平台的"交互气质"有可感的不同The two platforms feel different to interact with
谁更主动、谁更像创作伙伴——目前没定论，中立地测。Which leads more, which feels more like a creative partner — undecided, measured neutrally.
H3
整体创作体验对目标用户更好A better overall creative experience for the target user
H4
角色一致性 / 画面质量上有差异Differences in character consistency / visual quality

为什么这个结论可信Why the conclusion will be trustworthy

1

不靠一次印象No single impression

任何结论都要跨多个剧本、多名测试员——一次好用或难用，不算数。Every conclusion spans many scripts and testers — one good or bad run doesn't count.

2

两边喂一样的Identical inputs

同样的剧本、同样的话、同样的改法，两个平台逐字一致，对比才公平。Same scripts, same words, same edits — identical on both, so the comparison is fair.

3

让数据说话Data decides

视觉质量盲评打分，编辑能力逐条取证截图，不预设答案。Visuals scored blind; editing evidenced shot by shot. No preset answers.

一句话In one line

这套测试不会替你拍板"哪个更好"凭感觉，它会给你一份有证据、有样本量、可复查的对照结论——尤其是回答"新平台主打的编辑自由，到底兑现了没有"。This test won't decide "which is better" by feel — it hands you an evidenced, sample-sized, auditable comparison, above all answering whether the new platform's headline "editing freedom" actually delivered.
