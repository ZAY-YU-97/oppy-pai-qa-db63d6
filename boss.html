测试目的Objective

我们要回答的核心问题The core questions

开发者做新平台，最明确的一个目标是"给用户更多可编辑的自由"——剧本和图片都能更强地改。这是他们的赌注，也是这次测试的靶心。其余几条还没有定论，由数据来回答。The developer's clearest goal for the new platform is "more editing freedom for users" — stronger editing of both script and images. That's their bet and the bullseye of this test. The rest are still open, to be answered by data.

H1
Oppy 给用户更多可编辑自由Oppy gives users more editing freedom
到底成不成立？自由体现在哪？代价是什么？——重点验证。Does it hold? Where does the freedom show? At what cost? — the primary focus.
H2
两个平台的"交互气质"有可感的不同The two platforms feel different to interact with
谁更主动、谁更像创作伙伴——目前没定论，中立地测。Which leads more, which feels more like a creative partner — undecided, measured neutrally.
H3
整体创作体验对目标用户更好A better overall creative experience for the target user
H4
角色一致性 / 画面质量上有差异Differences in character consistency / visual quality
