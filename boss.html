测试目的Objective

我们要回答的核心问题The core questions

这次测试要回答的，不是"系统能不能生成一个漂亮的结果"，而是——经过反复编辑与迭代，创作者最终能不能做出自己心目中想要的影片。开发者最看重的能力是可编辑性（Editability）：真实创作者往往花数小时反复打磨参考素材、分镜与视频，所以靶心是这条"持续编辑 → 逐步逼近目标"的路径，而非一次成片的观感。下面三组维度，第一组最重要；两个平台用同一把尺子量，胜负由数据回答。The question isn't "can the system generate a pretty result," but — through repeated editing and iteration, can the creator finally make the film they have in mind. The capability the developer values most is Editability: real creators spend hours refining references, storyboard and video, so the bullseye is the "keep editing → converge on the goal" path, not the polish of a one-shot result. Of the three groups below, the first matters most; both platforms are measured with the same ruler, and the verdict comes from data.

A
创作控制与编辑能力 · 最重要Creative control & editing · most important
意图理解 · 编辑自由度 · 控制感 · 收敛速度 · 修改成本——开发者的核心赌注，重点验证。Intent understanding · editing freedom · sense of control · convergence speed · cost of change — the developer's core bet, verified hardest.
B
创作者工作流Creator workflow
Reference 管理 · Storyboard 调整 · Script→Video 对齐 · Agent 引导品质。Reference management · storyboard editing · Script→Video alignment · agent guidance.
C
最终作品品质Final output quality
故事完整性 · 画面品质 · 角色一致性。Story completeness · visual quality · character consistency.
