编辑自由度怎么打How to score editing freedom

打这一项时，自己对剧本和图片改几处（两平台改一样的），心里过这几点：能不能改、能改多细、几种改法、能不能撤销/回退、是用户接管还是只能请 AI、改完准不准（有没有外溢）、代价多大。一句话依据 + 分数。To score this, make a few edits of your own to the script and images (same on both platforms) and weigh: can it change, how fine, how many ways, can you undo/revert, do you take over or just ask the AI, is it precise (any bleed), at what cost. One-line rationale + score.

开放题（两平台分别答，限 200 字内）Open questions (per platform, ≤ 200 words)

  1. 最顺的一步 / 最受阻的一步是什么？The smoothest step / the most blocked step?
  2. 只能选一个平台做真实项目，你选哪个？为什么？If you could keep only one platform for real work, which, and why?
  3. 你期待存在、但两个平台都没有的功能？A feature you wanted that neither platform has?
  4. （差异化）你觉得 Oppy 在"编辑自由"上和老平台具体差在哪一步？说一个最具体的瞬间。(Differentiation) Where exactly does Oppy's "editing freedom" differ from the old platform? Name one concrete moment.
