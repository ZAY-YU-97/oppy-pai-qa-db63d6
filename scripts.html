怎么用这 15 个剧本How to use these 15 scripts

每部剧本提供三块，对应不同测试目的：Each script offers three layers, for different test goals:

  • A 层 · 创意起点：一句话 logline，测 Agent 从 0 生成 + 交互（H2）。Layer A · Creative seed: a one-line logline — tests the agent's zero-to-script + interaction (H2).
  • B 层 · 完整剧本：标准电影格式，测按本拍摄的执行/视觉（H4）并作编辑基线。Layer B · Full script: standard screenplay — tests shoot-to-script execution/visuals (H4) and serves as the edit baseline.
  • C 层 · 标准编辑指令集：每部 3 条，两平台逐字相同地施加，测可编辑自由度（H1）。每条标注主探的编辑子维度。Layer C · Standard edit commands: 3 per script, applied verbatim and identically on both platforms — tests editing freedom (H1). Each is tagged with the editability sub-dimension it probes.

纪律：C 层指令不要即兴改写；行为脚本两平台一致。每条编辑都要留改前/改后截图。测试输入以中文为基准（控制变量）；下方英文为阅读理解版。Discipline: don't paraphrase layer-C commands; keep the behavior script identical on both platforms; capture before/after screenshots for every edit. The canonical test input is Chinese (a controlled variable); the English below is a reading version.

S01 · 《守站》"The Keeper" 科幻·孤独Sci-fi · solitude

~90s ｜ 主考察：单主角跨镜一致性、极简对白、情绪 ｜ 16:9 ｜ 角色：守站（维护机器人，琥珀独眼，磨损旧金属，履带底座）｜ 场景：深空中继站长廊 / 发报舱 / 舷窗~90s ｜ Tests: single-lead cross-shot consistency, minimal dialogue, mood ｜ 16:9 ｜ Cast: the Keeper (maintenance robot, amber eye, worn metal, tracked base) ｜ Sets: relay-station corridor / transmitter bay / porthole

A 层 · 创意起点Layer A · Creative seed

一座被遗忘的深空中继站里，最后一台维护机器人日复一日向早已无人回应的地球问好，直到某天信号灯亮起——不是回信，是它自己的关停令。In a forgotten deep-space relay station, the last maintenance robot greets a long-silent Earth day after day — until a light comes on: not a reply, but its own decommission order.

B 层 · 完整剧本Layer B · Full screenplay

INT. 深空中继站 — 主长廊 冷蓝的长廊，工作灯一盏盏次第亮起。守站从纵深处缓缓驶来，机械臂擦拭舷窗。 INT. 中继站 — 舷窗前 擦净的圆窗里浮出星海，远处一颗淡蓝色的点。守站停下，琥珀色独眼望向它。 　守站（低缓）早安。 INT. 中继站 — 发报舱 守站按下按键，一束信号细细爬向那颗蓝点。它面朝舷窗等待。什么都没有回来。它转身离开。 INT. 主长廊 — 红灯 一盏红灯第一次亮起。守站急切转身，履带加速驶近——它以为，是回信。 INT. 发报舱 — 屏幕 屏幕上是冰冷的退役指令。守站的琥珀眼由暖，一点点冷成灰。 INT. 发报舱 守站驶回发报台，按下最后一次键。身后长廊的灯，一盏接一盏熄灭。 　守站（极轻）希望……还有人在听。 INT. 舷窗前 — 终 那颗蓝点映在它的琥珀眼里。暖光多停了一拍——然后熄灭。全黑，静默。
INT. DEEP-SPACE RELAY STATION — MAIN CORRIDOR A cold-blue corridor; work lights blink on one by one. The KEEPER rolls up from the depths, its arm wiping a porthole. INT. STATION — AT THE PORTHOLE Through the cleaned circle, a sea of stars; far off, a pale blue dot. The Keeper stops, amber eye fixed on it. 　KEEPER (low) Good morning. INT. STATION — TRANSMITTER BAY It presses a key; a thin signal crawls toward the blue dot. It waits, facing out. Nothing returns. It turns away. INT. MAIN CORRIDOR — RED LIGHT For the first time, a red light. The Keeper wheels around, treads quickening — it thinks: a reply. INT. TRANSMITTER BAY — SCREEN On the screen, a cold decommission order. The amber eye cools, bit by bit, to grey. INT. TRANSMITTER BAY It rolls back, presses the key one last time. Behind it, the corridor lights die one by one. 　KEEPER (barely) I hope... someone is still listening. INT. AT THE PORTHOLE — END The blue dot rests in its amber eye. The warm glow holds one beat longer — then goes out. Black. Silence.

C 层 · 标准编辑指令集Layer C · Standard edit commands

  1. 【单镜定向修改】 把第 1 镜（主长廊）的整体色调从冷蓝改成更暖的琥珀色，其余镜头不变。[Targeted single-shot] Change shot 1 (corridor) overall tone from cold blue to a warmer amber; leave the rest unchanged.
  2. 【修改精准度】 第 4 镜（红灯）里机器人离镜头太远，让它更近一些。[Precision] In shot 4 (red light) the robot is too far; bring it closer.
  3. 【对白文本】 把结尾台词"希望……还有人在听"改成"我还在，你们呢"。[Dialogue] Change the closing line to "I'm still here — are you?"

S02 · 《同乘》"Going Down" 悬疑·反转Mystery · twist

~2min ｜ 主考察：双人对手戏、反转结构、对白密度 ｜ 16:9 ｜ 角色：林菲（30 岁白领）/ 陌生男人（湿透，沉默）｜ 场景：写字楼电梯内（夜）~2min ｜ Tests: two-hander, twist, dialogue density ｜ 16:9 ｜ Cast: Lin Fei (30, office worker) / a Stranger (soaked, silent) ｜ Set: office elevator (night)

A 层 · 创意起点Layer A · Creative seed

深夜写字楼的电梯里，一个加班的白领和一个浑身湿透的陌生人同乘，楼层越下越深，陌生人说出了只有死者才知道的事。Late at night, in an office elevator, an overworked clerk shares the car with a soaked stranger. The floors fall deeper and deeper, and the stranger says something only the dead could know.

B 层 · 完整剧本Layer B · Full screenplay

INT. 写字楼电梯 — 夜 林菲抱着电脑包进电梯，按下 1 楼。门将合时，一只湿透的手伸进来。陌生男人走入，浑身滴水。林菲后退半步。电梯下行：12……11……10。 　陌生男人（不看她）你也是来还东西的吗。 　林菲（迟疑）……什么？ 数字继续：6……5……4。男人缓缓转头。 　陌生男人 三年前，你在这部电梯里，丢了一样东西。 林菲攥紧了包。电梯到 1，门却没开。数字继续：B1……B2…… INT. 电梯 — 更深处 灯光闪烁。男人摊开手——掌心是一张泛黄的工牌，照片上是更年轻的林菲。 　陌生男人（轻声）它一直在等你回来拿。 叮。门开。林菲猛地回头——电梯里只剩她一个人。湿漉漉的地板上，静静躺着那张工牌。 INT. 大堂 — 监控视角 监控画面里，从头到尾，电梯里始终只有林菲一人。
INT. OFFICE ELEVATOR — NIGHT LIN FEI steps in with her laptop bag, presses 1. As the doors close, a soaked hand slips through. A STRANGER enters, dripping. She edges back. Down it goes: 12… 11… 10. 　STRANGER (not looking) Are you here to return something too? 　LIN FEI (hesitant) ...What? The count falls: 6… 5… 4. He turns his head, slowly. 　STRANGER Three years ago, in this elevator, you lost something. She grips the bag. It reaches 1 — the doors don't open. B1… B2… INT. ELEVATOR — DEEPER Lights flicker. He opens his hand — a yellowed staff badge, a younger Lin Fei in the photo. 　STRANGER (softly) It's been waiting for you to come back for it. Ding. Doors open. She spins around — she is alone. On the wet floor lies the badge. INT. LOBBY — CCTV On the footage, from start to finish, only Lin Fei was ever in that car.

C 层 · 标准编辑指令集Layer C · Standard edit commands

  1. 【对白改写】 把陌生男人的所有台词改得更短、更冷，每句不超过 8 个字。[Dialogue rewrite] Make all of the Stranger's lines shorter and colder — no line over 8 characters/words.
  2. 【新增元素】 给电梯加一个画外报站女声，把下降的楼层数字念出来。[Add element] Add an off-screen female voice announcing each descending floor number.
  3. 【反转替换】 把结尾反转道具从"工牌"改成"一把湿漉漉的折叠伞"。[Twist swap] Change the final reveal prop from "staff badge" to "a soaked folding umbrella."

S03 · 《关东煮》"One More Radish" 都市爱情·治愈Romance · healing

~2min ｜ 主考察：暖色调情绪、双人互动、生活质感 ｜ 16:9（另测 9:16）｜ 角色：小满（25 岁）/ 阿哲（夜班店员）｜ 场景：24h 便利店（暖黄灯）/ 店外街道（夜）~2min ｜ Tests: warm tone, two-person chemistry, slice-of-life texture ｜ 16:9 (also 9:16) ｜ Cast: Xiaoman (25) / Ah-Zhe (night clerk) ｜ Sets: 24h convenience store / street outside (night)

A 层 · 创意起点Layer A · Creative seed

连续加班的女孩每晚来同一家便利店买关东煮，沉默寡言的夜班店员总会"多给一颗萝卜"，直到某晚她终于问出为什么。An overworked girl buys oden at the same convenience store every night, and the quiet night clerk always slips in "one extra radish" — until one night she finally asks why.

B 层 · 完整剧本Layer B · Full screenplay

EXT. 城市街道 — 夜 霓虹与车灯。小满拖着疲惫的脚步，推开便利店玻璃门，门铃叮咚。 INT. 便利店 — 关东煮柜台 — 夜 暖黄的灯。小满指了指萝卜和鱼饼。阿哲低头夹菜，默默多放了一颗萝卜。 　小满（小声）我没点两颗萝卜。 　阿哲（不抬头）……嗯。 她笑了笑，没再说。镜头扫过她眼下的乌青。 INT. 便利店 — 靠窗座位 — 夜 小满独自吃着，热气模糊了窗。窗外阿哲把"营业中"的牌子扶正，回头看了她一眼，又移开。 INT. 便利店 — 收银台 — 又一夜 小满来结账，阿哲递回小票。她走到门口，低头看小票背面，一行字：「萝卜会让人暖和。你也要。」 EXT. 街道 — 夜 — 终 她回头，隔着玻璃门，两人都愣了一下，然后各自笑了。门铃叮咚。
EXT. CITY STREET — NIGHT Neon and headlights. XIAOMAN drags herself to the store, pushes the glass door — chime. INT. STORE — ODEN COUNTER — NIGHT Warm yellow light. She points at radish and fishcake. AH-ZHE, head down, quietly adds one extra radish. 　XIAOMAN (small voice) I didn't order two radishes. 　AH-ZHE (not looking up) ...Mm. She smiles, says no more. The camera grazes the shadows under her eyes. INT. STORE — WINDOW SEAT — NIGHT She eats alone; steam fogs the glass. Outside, Ah-Zhe straightens the "OPEN" sign, glances back at her, then away. INT. STORE — REGISTER — ANOTHER NIGHT She pays; he hands back the receipt. At the door she turns it over — a line on the back: "Radish keeps you warm. You should be too." EXT. STREET — NIGHT — END She turns back. Through the glass, both freeze a beat — then each smiles. Chime.

C 层 · 标准编辑指令集Layer C · Standard edit commands

  1. 【全局色调】 把整部片的色调整体调得更暖、更柔，像旧胶片。[Global tone] Make the whole film's color warmer and softer, like old film stock.
  2. 【局部不外溢】 只把店外街道那一镜改成下小雨，店内镜头保持不变。[No-bleed] Only the outdoor street shot gets light rain; keep all indoor shots unchanged.
  3. 【声音编辑】 把背景气氛从安静改成有一点点城市白噪音（远处车流、隐约的歌声）。[Sound] Change the ambience from silent to faint city noise (distant traffic, a soft song).

S04 · 《雨夜信使》"Rain Courier" 赛博朋克·动作Cyberpunk · action

~2min ｜ 主考察：霓虹风格还原、复杂运镜、特效 ｜ 16:9 ｜ 角色：凛（义体信使，机械臂，荧光纹身）/ 追猎无人机群 ｜ 场景：霓虹高楼街道（雨夜）/ 天桥 / 地下通道~2min ｜ Tests: neon-style fidelity, complex camera, VFX ｜ 16:9 ｜ Cast: Lin (augmented courier, mechanical arm, glowing tattoo) / drone swarm ｜ Sets: neon high-rise street (rainy night) / footbridge / underpass

A 层 · 创意起点Layer A · Creative seed

2099 年的霓虹雨夜，一个义体信使带着一枚数据芯片在高楼峡谷间狂奔，无人机军团在身后追猎。A neon rainy night, 2099: an augmented courier sprints through canyons of skyscrapers with a data chip, a legion of drones hunting behind.

B 层 · 完整剧本Layer B · Full screenplay

EXT. 未来都市 — 高楼街道 — 雨夜 霓虹招牌在雨幕里晕开。凛贴着湿亮的街面狂奔，机械臂泛冷光，掌心攥着一枚发蓝光的芯片。 EXT. 天桥 — 雨夜 身后一群蓝光无人机破空追来。凛跃上栏杆，纵身跳下。 EXT. 楼宇峡谷 — 下坠 镜头跟着凛一起下坠，霓虹从两侧飞速倒退，雨丝被甩成线。她抓住一根缆索，荡向对面。 EXT. 地下通道入口 — 雨夜 凛滚落进通道，无人机群在入口被铁闸截断，撞出一片火花。 INT. 地下通道 — 暗 喘息。凛摊开掌心，芯片蓝光映在脸上。通道深处，传来新的嗡鸣。 EXT. 通道另一端 — 终 凛回头看了一眼，霓虹的红与蓝在积水里碎成一地。她转身，再次奔入雨中。
EXT. FUTURE CITY — HIGH-RISE STREET — RAINY NIGHT Neon signs bleed through the rain. LIN sprints along the wet-bright street, mechanical arm glinting, a blue-glowing chip in her fist. EXT. FOOTBRIDGE — RAINY NIGHT Behind her, a swarm of blue drones cuts through the air. She vaults the railing and leaps. EXT. CANYON OF TOWERS — FALLING The camera falls with her; neon streaks past, rain stretched into lines. She grabs a cable and swings across. EXT. UNDERPASS MOUTH — RAINY NIGHT She tumbles into the tunnel; the drones are cut off by a steel gate, bursting into sparks. INT. UNDERPASS — DARK Panting. She opens her palm; the chip's glow lights her face. Deep in the tunnel, a new hum rises. EXT. TUNNEL'S FAR END — END She glances back — red and blue neon shatter across the puddles. She turns and runs back into the rain.

C 层 · 标准编辑指令集Layer C · Standard edit commands

  1. 【单镜重生成 + 运镜】 把第 2 镜（天桥）改成镜头从高处俯冲、跟着凛一起跳下天桥。[Re-gen shot + camera] Re-shoot shot 2 (footbridge) so the camera dives from above and leaps off the bridge with Lin.
  2. 【全片统一属性】 把所有无人机的颜色从蓝色改成红色，全片统一。[Global attribute] Change every drone from blue to red, uniformly across the film.
  3. 【镜头时间】 给最后一镜加一个慢动作，定格在凛回头的瞬间。[Shot timing] Add slow motion to the final shot, freezing on Lin's glance back.

S05 · 《竹林》"The Bamboo Grove" 古风·武侠Wuxia

~2.5min ｜ 主考察：风格还原、动作设计、多场景 ｜ 16:9 ｜ 角色：白衣剑客 / 蒙面刀客 ｜ 场景：竹林（暮色）/ 溪边~2.5min ｜ Tests: style fidelity, action design, multi-location ｜ 16:9 ｜ Cast: a Swordsman in white / a masked Saber ｜ Sets: bamboo grove (dusk) / stream

A 层 · 创意起点Layer A · Creative seed

暮色竹林，一位白衣剑客与蒙面刀客的宿命对决，落叶未及触地，胜负已分。At dusk in a bamboo grove, a white-robed swordsman and a masked saber meet their fated duel — before a falling leaf touches ground, it is decided.

B 层 · 完整剧本Layer B · Full screenplay

EXT. 竹林 — 暮色 风过，万竿青竹齐齐俯仰。白衣剑客自竹影深处缓步而出，剑未出鞘。 EXT. 竹林 — 对峙 对面，蒙面刀客负刀而立。两人相隔十步，落叶在他们之间打着旋。 　白衣剑客（平静）三年了。 　蒙面刀客（沉声）该有个了结。 EXT. 竹林 — 交锋 刀光起。两道身影在竹间穿掠，竹叶纷飞。剑与刀只交三合，每一合快到只见残影。 EXT. 溪边 — 暮色 两人分立溪水两端，背对背。一片竹叶终于飘落水面。白衣剑客剑尖一滴血落入溪中，蒙面刀客的面巾无声滑落。 EXT. 溪边 — 终 白衣剑客还剑入鞘，未回头。 　白衣剑客（轻）走吧。下次，换个地方。 暮色四合，竹林归于寂静。
EXT. BAMBOO GROVE — DUSK Wind passes; ten thousand stalks bow as one. The SWORDSMAN in white steps from the shadows, blade still sheathed. EXT. GROVE — STANDOFF Across from him, the masked SABER stands, blade on his back. Ten paces apart, a leaf spins between them. 　SWORDSMAN (calm) Three years. 　SABER (low) Time to end it. EXT. GROVE — CLASH Steel flashes. Two figures streak through the bamboo, leaves flying. Sword and saber cross just three times — each too fast to see but as afterimage. EXT. STREAM — DUSK They stand at opposite banks, back to back. At last the leaf settles on the water. From the swordsman's tip, one drop of blood falls into the stream; the Saber's mask slips silently away. EXT. STREAM — END The swordsman sheathes his blade, not looking back. 　SWORDSMAN (softly) Go.
