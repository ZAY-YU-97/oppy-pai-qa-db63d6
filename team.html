怎么测：三步走How to test: three steps

用同一套剧本喂两个平台，填两张表，bug 发 Notion。重点测一件事——新平台主打的"编辑自由"到底兑现了没有。Feed both platforms the same scripts, fill two sheets, file bugs in Notion. Above all, test one thing — whether the new platform's headline "editing freedom" actually delivered.

怎么做How it works

01

同样地喂两个平台Feed both the same

取一个剧本，按 A/B 两层、逐字相同地喂给 Oppy 和 PAI；测编辑自由度时，自己拟几处修改，两边改一样的。Take a script; feed its A/B layers verbatim and identically to Oppy and PAI; for editing freedom, make a few edits of your own and apply the same ones to both.

02

填两张表Fill two sheets

① 评分表（含编辑自由度）② 差异化体验表。先写一句依据再打分，先独立后讨论。① the score sheet (incl. editing freedom) ② the experience sheet. Rationale before score; independent first, discuss later.

03

Bug 发 NotionBugs to Notion

发现功能性问题，按既有格式发到 Notion 测试反馈区，附截图/录屏。Functional issues go to the Notion test-feedback area in the existing format, with screenshot/recording.

两条公平原则Two fairness rules

两边喂一样的——语言/措辞/节奏一致，否则测的是你不是平台。顺序轮换——A 组先 Oppy 后 PAI，B 组反过来，抵消"先测的占便宜"。同一剧本同一测试员两平台都跑，才算一组对比。Identical inputs — same language/wording/pace, or you measure the tester, not the platform. Rotate order — Group A does Oppy-then-PAI, Group B the reverse. Only same-script, same-tester, both-platform runs form a pair.

评分什么What to score

评分表里 1–5 打这些维度，先写一句依据再打分。视觉类尽量并排盲评（隐去平台名）。Score these on the sheet, 1–5, rationale before number. Score visuals blind & side-by-side where possible.

编辑自由度 = 靶心Editing freedom = bullseye

这是整次测试的重点。打分时自己对剧本和图片改几处（两平台改一样的），过这几点：能不能改 · 能改多细 · 几种改法 · 能不能撤销回退 · 是接管还是只能请 AI · 改完准不准（有没有外溢）· 代价多大。详细打分说明见 交付表单 ①。This is the focus. When scoring, make a few edits of your own to the script and images (same on both platforms) and weigh: can it change · how fine · how many ways · undo/revert · take-over or just ask · precise or bleeding · at what cost. Full guidance in deliverable sheet ①.

差异化怎么测How to test differentiation

H1

编辑自由（重点）Editing freedom (focus)

同一剧本两平台各出一版片，再施加完全相同的几处修改（自己拟，两边一致），逐条记「能否做到 / 准不准 / 能否退 / 代价」+ 改前改后截图。这是开发者的核心主张，重点验证。One cut per platform from the same script, then apply the same edits to both, logging "done / precise / reversible / cost" + before-after shots. This is the developer's core claim — verify it hardest.

H2

交互气质（中立观测）Interaction (neutral)

谁更主动、谁更像创作伙伴，目前没定论。行为脚本两边一致，把感受写进差异化体验表 + 开放题，不靠一次印象下结论。Which leads more, which feels more like a partner — undecided. Keep the behavior script identical, write impressions into the experience sheet + open questions, no verdict from one run.

Oppy 内测会失败/重试，是已知现象——如实记录、单列，别因此整体压低评分；功能性 bug 才发 Notion。Oppy's beta will fail/retry — known and expected. Record it, but don't dock the overall score; only functional bugs go to Notion.

你要交什么What you hand in

①

评分表Score sheet

每个「剧本 × 测试员」一份，两平台并列，含编辑自由度。One per "script × tester," both platforms, incl. editing freedom.

②

差异化体验表Experience sheet

Likert + 开放题，先独立填后讨论。Likert + open questions, independent first.

③

Notion BugNotion bugs

功能性问题按格式发 Notion，附截图/录屏。Functional issues to Notion in-format, with media.

另外：每个剧本两平台的成片/录屏留档，编辑自由度的改前/改后截图留好，作为证据。结论要标样本量，样本不足就写"数据不足"。Also: keep each script's cuts/recordings and the before/after editing screenshots as evidence. State sample size in conclusions; if thin, write "insufficient data."
