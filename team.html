怎么测：选 1 部，像创作者一样深耕How to test: pick one, go deep like a creator

不追求数量——每人挑 1 个剧本，花几小时反复打磨。先备好参考素材，再在 References / Storyboard / Video 三个环节不断编辑、迭代，验证一件事：经过反复编辑，你最终能不能做出心里想要的影片。两个平台同样地做，填两张表，bug 发 Notion。Not about volume — each person picks one script and spends hours refining it. Prepare references first, then keep editing and iterating across References / Storyboard / Video to test one thing: after repeated editing, can you finally make the film you have in mind. Do the same on both platforms, fill two sheets, file bugs in Notion.

怎么做How it works

01

选 1 部 + 先备 ReferencePick one + prep references

从 15 部里挑 1 部你想深做的。开工前，自己先收集参考素材：角色 / 场景 / 风格·情绪 / 镜头四类，越接近真实创作越好。Choose one of the 15 to take deep. Before starting, gather your own references — character / location / style·mood / shot — as close to real creation as possible.

02

两平台同样地做Do the same on both

同一剧本、同样的参考与措辞，分别在 Oppy 和 PAI 走一遍；编辑时两边施加完全相同的改动。Same script, same references and wording, run once on Oppy and once on PAI; apply the exact same edits to both.

03

三环节反复编辑Edit across three stages

在 References / Storyboard / Video 每个环节都主动修改、调整、重新生成、比较方案——别看到还行就进下一步。每轮留改前/改后截图。At References / Storyboard / Video, actively modify, adjust, regenerate, compare — don't move on at the first "good enough." Keep before/after shots each round.

04

填表 + 发 BugFill sheets + file bugs

两平台都做完，填① 评分表 + ② 体验表（先写依据再打分，先独立后讨论）；功能性问题按格式发 Notion，附截图/录屏。After both, fill ① score + ② experience sheets (rationale before score, independent first); functional issues to Notion in-format with media.

开工前：先备好 ReferenceBefore you start: prepare references

真实创作者动手前都会先攒一批参考素材。选定剧本后，自己先收集四类参考：角色（Character）· 场景/地点（Location）· 风格/情绪（Style·Mood）· 镜头（Shot）。这样测出来的"可编辑性"才贴近真实使用情境。Real creators gather references before they begin. After choosing your script, collect four kinds yourself: Character · Location · Style·Mood · Shot. This makes the "editability" you measure reflect real usage.

两条公平原则Two fairness rules

两边喂一样的——语言/措辞/节奏一致，否则测的是你不是平台。顺序轮换——A 组先 Oppy 后 PAI，B 组反过来，抵消"先测的占便宜"。同一剧本同一测试员两平台都跑，才算一组对比。Identical inputs — same language/wording/pace, or you measure the tester, not the platform. Rotate order — Group A does Oppy-then-PAI, Group B the reverse. Only same-script, same-tester, both-platform runs form a pair.

评分什么What to score

评分表按开发者的三组框架，1–5 打分，先写一句依据再打分。A 组最重要；视觉类尽量并排盲评（隐去平台名）。The sheet follows the developer's three groups, scored 1–5, rationale before number. Group A matters most; score visuals blind & side-by-side where possible.

整次测试的重点不是"能不能一次生成漂亮结果"，而是能不能通过反复编辑、逐步逼近你想要的成片。在 References / Storyboard / Video 三环节主动改（两平台改一样的），过这几点：懂不懂意图 · 能不能改并保留好的部分 · 方向能否精准控制 · 几轮后是否更接近目标 · 要重做几次。详细说明见 交付表单 ①。The focus isn't "can it generate a pretty result in one shot," but whether repeated editing converges on the cut you want. 差异化怎么测How to test differentiation

A

编辑与迭代（重点）Editing & iteration (focus)

同一剧本两平台各做一遍，在 References / Storyboard / Video 施加完全相同的修改（自己拟，两边一致），逐条记「能否做到 / 准不准 / 能否退 / 代价」+ 几轮后是否收敛 + 改前改后截图。这是开发者的核心主张，重点验证。Run the same script on both, apply the exact same edits across References / Storyboard / Video, logging "done / precise / reversible / cost" + whether it converges over rounds + before/after shots. The developer's core claim — verify it hardest.

B

工作流与 Agent 引导（中立观测）Workflow & agent guidance (neutral)

参考素材、分镜是否好建好改，Agent 的建议/提问是否有价值——哪个更顺没有预设。行为脚本两边一致，把感受写进体验表 + 开放题，不靠一次印象下结论。Whether references and storyboard are easy to build & edit, and whether the agent's suggestions/questions add value — no preset on which is smoother. Keep the behavior script identical, write impressions into the experience sheet + open questions, no verdict from one run.
